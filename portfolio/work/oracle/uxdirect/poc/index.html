User Checklist

Use our easy-to-use checklist to get to know your users:

  1. A user experience champion is needed to drive change from technically-focused to user-focused implementations

    Do you have a user experience champion at the decision making level?  

  2. IT developers and analysts do not represent the real user populations. Get representative users as part of the team

    Are real users represented in the implementation?  

  3. User profiles help define the important characteristics of your end users and what their goals are?

    Have you created user profiles for your target users?  

    Use user profile template to create your user profiles.

  4. Understanding user’s tasks and how frequently they perform those tasks helps inform designs.

    Have you defined user’s tasks by role and frequency of use?  

    Our task matrix can help you analyze how frequently users perform each task

  5. Users are not good in articulating how their work can be improved. To understand their needs it is always best to observe how they do their work.

    Have you understood the user’s pain points and needs and wants?  

    Our Needs and Wants template can help you gather this information

  6. The goal of the new application is always to optimize work and make users productive.

    Have you created new business task flows which optimize how the users work today?  

    Use our Task Flow model template to define the new workflow
