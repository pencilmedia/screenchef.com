Design an Experience

User Experience is a critical factor that decides successful user adoption of enterprise applications. Oracle's Application Experience team takes pride in developing application user experiences which are useful, usable and productive.

When you tailor your enterprise application designs to meet your business needs it is critical you follow our UX principles and best practices to maintain or enhance the user experience

Once you have identified your user needs, you are now ready to conceptualize the designs and apply design best practices. Use our best practices and design toolkit to enhance your user experiences.

Setting Usability Goals

Once you have collected information about your user's needs you can define qualitative or quantitative usability goals for your enterprise applications.

You can apply user performance criteria for your implementation:

  •  Effectiveness: How easily can the user complete their core tasks without any help desk calls?
    Example of Goal: Reduce Help Desk Calls by 50%
  •  Efficiency: How long it takes the user to complete the core tasks?
    Example of Goal: Reduced time to complete the core tasks by 30%
  •  Satisfaction: How happy and satisfied the users are in performing their core tasks?
    Example of Goal: User satisfaction rating of 60% or grater.

Principles to great Enterprise applications

Make your enterprise applications:

  • 1 Useful - makes users achieve their goals easily.
  • 2 Productive - makes users more efficient in their work.
  • 3 Contextual - provide the right information at the right time to the users.
  • 4 Simple yet effective - hide the complexity which the users don't need to see.

UX Patterns & Guidelines

Design patterns describe common flows or page designs that are used within an application. They are built according to common requirements using industry best practices and tested for usability. These design patterns, used internally by Oracle developers during the development process, are now available to you, ensuring consistent and compelling user experiences to your end users when customizing your applications

To ensure consistency and rapid development of your customized solutions it is critical you adopt our product design patterns and guidelines. Design patterns are proven design solutions to how user’s search, navigate and perform their tasks using web applications. Oracle User Experience team have designed and tested these design patterns. The goal is your customized designs should resemble the behavior of Oracle applications so users don’t have to learn new ways of interacting with the customized solutions.

Follow our product specific design patterns to for common design approaches.

Fusion Guidelines Standards and Patterns

The Fusion Guidelines Standards and Patterns are provided to help you understand the key design concepts of Oracle Fusion Applications. These are top design patterns and guidelines you should be aware of when modifying or extending Fusion applications. Use these guidelines to create your own Dashboards, Navigation modules and Work areas suited to your end user needs. Applying these guidelines and patterns will ensure your extensions and customizations are consistent with the rest of the Oracle Fusion applications.
