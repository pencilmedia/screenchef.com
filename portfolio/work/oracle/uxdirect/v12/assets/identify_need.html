Enterprise Application Users

Customers of enterprise applications now expect "consumer-like" user experience as a key differentiator. Typically, enterprise application implementation is a major expense and undertaking for any customer. The goal is to get return through automation, end-user productivity and business efficiency. Use our toolkit to collect the right information about your users.

Who are your users?

A simple question but often the knowledge of the users is assumed by the implementation developers. Knowing your users in the changing workplace is essential to creating implementation solutions. Use our toolkit to conduct users research and understand your users. Listen to their needs, observe them in the their work content and empathize with them. Involve the users in the implementation cycle for building successful enterprise applications.

What is the context of their work?

Users cannot easily articulate needs. It's the context of what they are doing that tell us what they are trying to do and what they need. Observing user's tasks, the tools they use and their ecosystem helps us define the problem and solutions they need. Use our toolkit to define user's needs and wants.
