Baseline Current Implementation Usability

Measure how usable the current app is by ask employees to perform their typical task and record the result. Measure how easy it is for employees complete the job without help (effectiveness), how long it takes employees complete a task (efficiency), and do they leave work happy using the application in their job (satisfaction).
