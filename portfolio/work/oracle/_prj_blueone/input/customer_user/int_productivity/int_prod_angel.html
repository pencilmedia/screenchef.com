TEDIUM

1. What are the most tedious / time consuming tasks you do in YOUR JOB? Is there anything Agile can do to help streamline this?
Getting Supplier data sheets and ROHS information.  Typical CE activity - new manufacturers and 2nd sources. Go through qualification processes.  Google search to find data.  Print datasheets and compare information to make part selections. 

2. What are the most tedious / time consuming tasks you do IN AGILE? What should Agile change in the product to fix this?
Training people how to use the system.  This includes Agile features as well as the company business processes.  Training on reports and searches.  Learning the whole system.
Changing Workflows as signoffs change.
Putting in ROHS attachments.  30 to 40 PDFs for Power Supplies.  tedious to manage all the data.

HAND DATA ENTRY CASES

1. Describe the process you follow to enter PARTS by hand. What works well in Agile? What about Agile makes this difficult?

Use Save As mostly.  Use intelligent part numbers, so have to scroll through the used numbers and pick the right Dash number.

Create Date does not revert to default on Save As.

2. Describe the process you follow to enter / modify BOMs by hand. What works well in Agile? What about Agile makes this difficult?

Save AS and Import mainly used.  Happy with ECO redline process.  Use ECOs for AML and BOM changes. 

With a re-spin, must restructure many many BOMs. One CCA is used in dozens of assemblies.  Changing the FAB will generate new TLA dash numbers.

3. Describe your change management process? How are BOMs, AMLs, Drawings changed during the ECO process? What works well in Agile? What about Agile makes this difficult?

One data entry person for CCA.  Only 2 hardware engineers, but many software engineers.  Process allows for prototype release with few drawings, then need more docs as Lifecycle moves up.  With lifecycle changes, the doc requirements are stricter. 

SEARCH

1. What do you do with search results once presented on the screen? How do you use them? What works well in Agile? What about Agile makes this difficult?

Get results and dump to Access for analysis.  Limiting to 1000 or 5000 would break the process.
