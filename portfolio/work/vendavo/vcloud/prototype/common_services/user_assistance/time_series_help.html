User Assistance: Time Series

What is plotted on this chart?

The Time Series Chart presents the trend over time for your transactions. The goal is to understand if certain events occurred during the year which impacted your margin, like the implementation of a new rebate program in July or a new product launch in November.

What is the hover detail telling me?

When you hover over a specific dot in the chart, you can see the number of transactions in that month which are a percentage of Market Price for the root cause. Use the View by Product selector to the right to see the performance of transactions for a particular product.

What can I do to improve the red transactions?

Review the content available at the top of the Opportunity Detail page for recommended actions to resolve the root cause of the profit leak. The trend may show you behavior of which you are already aware; however, you may see where the creation or modification of a policy can improve the profitability of your bad transactions.
